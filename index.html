Klippr

a meego client for Kippt.com.

for the Nokia N9 and the N950.

Download .deb View on GitHub

With kippt.com you can organize, archive and search your links.

Current version (Beta)

* 0.4.0 - 07.09.12

The stable version will get released in the Ovi Store.

Features:

  • organize your boomarks in lists.
  • edit your bookmarks.
  • read your bookmarks in an optimized reader view.
  • follow your feed.

Not yet implemented:

  • Search
  • Userviews
  • Better sharing option
  • packages for nemomobile and Mer
